📄 RESUME INSTRUCTIONS
======================

To enable the "Download Resume" button on your portfolio:

1. Create or export your resume as a PDF file
2. Rename it to: resume.pdf
3. Place it in this folder: frontend/public/
4. The download button will automatically work!

Your resume will be downloaded as: "Getanew_Tilahun_Resume.pdf"

Note: Make sure the file is named exactly "resume.pdf" (lowercase)
